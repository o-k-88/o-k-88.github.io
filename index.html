About

Oleh Kyrylenko

Front End Webdeveloper since 2019.

Hi there, my name is Oleh and I'm from Ukraine. I work primarily in HTML, CSS and JavaScript. I learned them on my own using both free and paid training resources.

Most recently, I worked as Web Developer at K&K Provitrum, where my main responsibilities were to manage and update content, as well as to design and establish user-friendly website, including responsive design. As part of this project, I also worked with Wordpress and PHP.

Prior to that, I work on the CryptoMender web app as a User Acquisition Lead and UX/UI Designer. While testing and improving the design of the app, as well designing advertising banners and the logo, I also worked with Figma, PhotoShop, and InDesign.

While working on IT projects I focus on the efficiency of execution, which I learned while professionally playing and coaching soccer in the US, Spain and my native Ukraine. Web development started as my hobby, but over the last four years, it has truly become my profession.
